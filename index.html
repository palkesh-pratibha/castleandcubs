About the Channel

A learning space designed for children and the adults who guide them.

Castle & Cubs shares educational content that makes learning fun, interactive, and easy to understand. The focus is on helping children grow through practical lessons, engaging activities, and creative teaching methods that support everyday development.

  • Educational videos designed to be engaging and easy to follow
  • Montessori-based ideas that encourage hands-on learning
  • Simple resources that parents and educators can apply at home or in class
